# DCE-NAM-Campaign
DCS World DCE Vietnam Campaign 
NAM 1965-74 - This is a semi-historical campaign meant to represent a certain vision of the Vietnam War. The campaign is built around the F-4E Phantom, but other aircrafts or helicopters are available for both single-player and multiplayer campaigns, with various aircraft types and missions. Aircraft and weaponry will evolve throughout this campaign, as will the authorized targets. Even if the rules of engagement do not strictly follow historical chronology, they allow us to feel the frustration they must have caused at the time for the aviators who faced them.
